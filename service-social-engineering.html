Social Engineering & Awareness Testing

Technical defenses matter, but attackers still rely heavily on people. This engagement helps you understand how staff respond to realistic but controlled social engineering attempts, and how to build long-term awareness.

What This Service Covers

  • Authorized phishing simulations tailored to your environment.
  • Scenarios aligned with real-world attacker tactics, not scare tactics.
  • Measurement of click rates, reporting rates, and response patterns.
  • Optional follow-up education materials or debrief sessions.

How the Engagement Works

  • We define clear boundaries, objectives, and communication plans.
  • Campaigns are designed to be realistic but non-disruptive.
  • Only authorized scenarios are used; no surprises for stakeholders.
  • Results are anonymized where possible and used for improvement, not blame.

What You Learn

  • How likely staff are to engage with suspicious messages.
  • Which types of lures and pretexts are most effective.
  • Where training, communication, or process updates are needed.
  • Practical recommendations to strengthen your human defenses.

Is This Right for You?

Choose this service if you want to go beyond generic awareness training and measure how your organization responds to realistic, controlled scenarios.
