Referral program FAQ

How is the 10% calculated?

On the agreed project fee (excluding VAT, if applicable). If the client books a €1,200 pentest, your share is €120 once the invoice is paid.

Do I have to handle any of the pentesting work?

No. You stay in your lane (IT support, web dev, whatever you do best). I handle scoping, testing, reporting and calls about the pentest itself.

Can I stay in the loop with what you’re doing?

Yes. If the client is happy with it, I’m happy to keep you in CC on key emails and debrief calls so you can help them with follow-up changes.

What if a client comes back to you later for more work?

If the new work is a clear continuation of the original engagement you sent (e.g. a retest or follow-up scope for the same client), your 10% still applies. If it’s a completely new client or an unrelated project, I’ll always tell you where it stands so there’s no weirdness.
