Reporting & Remediation Support

A good assessment doesn’t end with a scan output. The real value comes from clear communication and practical guidance that your teams can act on. This service underpins every engagement I deliver.

What My Reporting Includes

  • Executive summary: what was tested, key risks, and business impact.
  • Technical findings: detailed descriptions, evidence, and affected assets.
  • Risk ratings: severity and priority to help you focus effort.
  • Remediation guidance: practical steps and references for fixing issues.

Remediation & Follow-Up Options

  • Walkthrough sessions for technical teams to discuss complex findings.
  • Clarification on recommendations and potential implementation approaches.
  • Re-testing of fixed issues where agreed.
  • Input into longer-term security roadmaps and future testing cycles.

Why This Matters

Many organizations already know they have issues somewhere. The value of a pentest is turning that uncertainty into a prioritized, understandable plan.

My goal is to leave you with more than a PDF — you should walk away with a clear sense of what to do next, who needs to be involved, and how to measure progress.

Ready to Turn Findings Into Actions?

Whether you’re planning your first assessment or building an ongoing testing program, I can help you go from “we had a pentest” to “we actually improved our security”.
