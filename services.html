Professional Pentesting Services

Every engagement is scoped to your environment and goals. I follow industry standards and provide clear, actionable outcomes.

Web Application Pentest

Full-stack testing of your web applications following OWASP Testing Guide principles. I focus on authentication, authorization, input validation, business logic abuse, and data exposure.

API Security Assessment

Testing REST/GraphQL APIs for insecure endpoints, broken access control, misconfigured auth, and data leakage across environments.

Network & Infrastructure Testing

Internal and external network assessments, service enumeration, credential attack resistance, and lateral movement simulation.

Social Engineering & Phishing

Controlled social engineering exercises and phishing simulations to test awareness and response, without causing operational damage.

Red Team Style Engagements

Goal-driven simulations that chain multiple weaknesses to measure real-world impact and test your detection and response capabilities.

Reporting & Remediation Support

Easy-to-read reports with risk ratings, evidence, and recommendations — plus optional follow-up sessions with your technical teams.
