Scenario-Based & Red Team Style Engagements

Sometimes you need more than a checklist-style assessment. Scenario-based and red team style engagements focus on realistic goals and attack paths to test how well your people, processes, and technology work together.

What This Service Focuses On

  • Goal-oriented testing (e.g. access to a specific system or data set).
  • Chaining multiple weaknesses into realistic attack paths.
  • Measuring detection, response, and communication between teams.
  • Highlighting strengths as well as gaps in your defenses.

How the Engagement Works

  • We define scope, goals, constraints, and safety rules in detail.
  • Testing is carried out carefully to avoid disruption to business operations.
  • Evidence is captured at each stage to show what was done and why.
  • Findings are presented as narratives and attack paths, not just a list of issues.

Outcomes You Can Expect

  • Clear view of how an attacker could realistically progress in your environment.
  • Insight into which alerts fired, which didn’t, and how they were handled.
  • Recommendations that combine technical fixes with process improvements.
  • Material you can use to train both security staff and leadership.

Is This Right for You?

This service is best for organizations that already have some security maturity and want to test how everything works under pressure, instead of only checking isolated systems.
